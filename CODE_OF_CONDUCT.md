# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Luz project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Show empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication related to Luz.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at **matiassfernandez00@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Violations may result in a warning, removal of contributions, or a permanent ban from the project, depending on the severity.
